# First Run

## Creating Your Vault

1. Launch SecuritySmith.
2. You will see the **Unlock Screen**. Since this is your first run, tap **Create Vault**.
3. Enter a strong master password (minimum 8 characters). This password encrypts your entire vault.
4. Write down your **12-word recovery phrase** exactly as shown. You will be asked to confirm 3 random words to prove you saved it.
5. Your vault is now ready.

## Recovery Phrase

- The recovery phrase is your only backup if you forget your password.
- It is shown **once** during setup.
- Store it offline — on paper, in a safe. Never store it in a cloud note or password manager.
- You can generate a new recovery phrase later from **Settings → Security**.

## Initial Settings

After unlocking, go to **Settings** to configure:
- **Profile** — your name and brand info
- **Appearance** — light or dark theme
- **AI** — URL for your local Ollama instance (default: `http://localhost:11434`)
- **Email** — SMTP settings for sending reports and invoices

## Warning

If you lose both your master password and your recovery phrase, your data **cannot** be recovered. There is no backdoor.
