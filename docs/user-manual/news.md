# Security News and Client Alerts

## RSS Feeds

SecuritySmith subscribes to security news feeds to keep you informed.

### Default Feeds

On first run, these feeds are added automatically:
- BleepingComputer
- The Hacker News
- CISA Alerts

You can remove them or add your own.

### Adding a Feed

1. Go to **News**.
2. Click **Add feed**.
3. Enter the RSS URL and a display name.
4. Save.

## Tech Stack Tagging

Tag each client with their technology stack:
- Open a client and add tags like `nginx`, `wordpress`, `aws`, `java`.
- The app matches incoming news articles against these tags.
- Matched articles are highlighted with the affected client name(s).

## Client Alerts

When a news article matches a client's tech stack, you can:
- Read the article
- Click **Send alert** to compose an email to the affected client
- Dismiss the alert if not relevant

## Pruning

Only the last 200 articles per feed are retained. Older articles are pruned automatically.
